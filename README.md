## Questions (KPIs)
Basic:
- Retrieve the total number of orders placed.
- Calculate the total revenue generated from pizza sales.
- Identify the highest-priced pizza.
- Identify the most common pizza size ordered.
- List the top 5 most ordered pizza types along with their quantities.

Intermediate:
- Join the necessary tables to find the total quantity of each pizza category ordered.
- Determine the distribution of orders by hour of the day.
- Join relevant tables to find the category-wise distribution of pizzas.
- Group the orders by date and calculate the average number of pizzas ordered per day.
- Determine the top 3 most ordered pizza types based on revenue.

Advanced:
- Calculate the percentage contribution of each pizza type to total revenue.
- Analyze the cumulative revenue generated over time.
- Determine the top 3 most ordered pizza types based on revenue for each pizza category.



## Project Insight

- Classic and large pizzas generate the highest sales and revenue.
- Margherita and Pepperoni are the most ordered and top-selling pizza types.
- Evening hours show the highest order volume, indicating peak demand.
- Revenue follows an 80/20 pattern‚Äîfew pizzas contribute most of the income.
- Monthly and cumulative trends show steady growth in overall sales.

## Final Conclusion:

The Pizza Sales Analysis shows that customer demand is strongly driven by classic flavors and larger pizza sizes, which generate most of the revenue. Peak sales occur in the evenings, indicating strong dinner-time ordering behavior. A small set of top-selling pizzas contributes to the majority of overall income, showing an 80/20 sales pattern. Monthly and cumulative trends reflect steady business growth, highlighting consistent customer engagement and strong sales performance throughout the period.
